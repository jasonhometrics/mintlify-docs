A mortgage lender is a financial institution that provides loans secured by real estate. Lenders evaluate borrower finances, determine loan amounts, and fund transactions at closing.

Most property purchases require financing. The lender selected affects interest rates, closing timelines, and how problems are handled during the transaction.
