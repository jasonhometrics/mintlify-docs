## Pilot Program

See results before you commit. We run a review reactivation campaign to a list of your recent clients so you can see exactly how the system works before making any commitment.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Discovery call">
    Confirm fit and align on your review goals.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit your client list">
    Provide up to 150 recent clients with valid contact info. No system integration required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="We run the campaign">
    Branded review requests go out via SMS and email over 10 days. We handle everything.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the results together">
    We walk you through response rates and review activity. You decide what comes next.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  No system integration required during the pilot. Full onboarding happens only after you decide to move forward.
</Note>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is this for individual agents or solo professionals?">
    Hometrics is built for businesses with consistent transaction volume. Most clients process 10+ transactions per month. Individual agents with low volume are generally not a fit.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need to be a Hometrics customer to be listed?">
    No. Any business can claim a free Starter listing on Hometrics. Your directory profile, leaderboard ranking, and Google review sync are all available at no cost. Paid tiers add review automation, campaign management, and done-for-you features on top of that foundation.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What exactly is done-for-you?">
    We manage the campaigns for you — building sequences, sending requests, handling follow-ups, and posting reviews to social. You don't log in and run anything. You provide access to your client list and we handle the rest.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I respond to my own reviews?">
    Yes. You always retain full access to your Google Business Profile. Respond anytime, or let us handle responses for you on the appropriate plan.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What CRMs do you integrate with?">
    We integrate with most CRMs and transaction management systems used in real estate. [Contact us](https://www.hometrics.ai/contact-us) before signing up to confirm compatibility with your system.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I cancel anytime?">
    Yes. All plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts unless you commit to an annual package. Your directory listing and leaderboard visibility remain active on the free Starter tier.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens after the free trial?">
    After 10 days you can choose to continue on a paid plan or drop back to the free Starter tier. No charge until you decide to move forward.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
