Legal issues touch every aspect of real estate. Buying, selling, renting, inheriting, investing, and resolving disputes all involve legal frameworks, rights, and protections.

This section covers consumer protections, when and how to work with attorneys, contracts and agreements, landlord-tenant relationships, estate planning, and common disputes.
