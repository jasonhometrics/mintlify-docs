## A Directory That Works for You

Hometrics is a public directory where buyers and sellers research real estate professionals based on verified reviews and business information. Claiming your profile puts you in control of how you appear.

---

## What You Get

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Performance Badges" icon="trophy">
    Earn trust signals automatically. Badges like [Local Legend](/overview/platform-features/performance-badges), [Top Rated](/overview/platform-features/performance-badges), and [Trending](/overview/platform-features/performance-badges) are awarded based on your Google review activity. Consumers see them when browsing the directory.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Differentiators" icon="sliders">
    Highlight what sets you apart. Weekend availability, free consultations, multi-lingual staff, wire fraud insurance, and other [differentiators](/overview/platform-features/differentiators) help consumers filter for businesses that match their needs.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Easy Contact Options" icon="address-book">
    Make it simple for prospects to reach you. Link your website, calendar, and quoting tools directly on your profile. Buyers and sellers can book a call or request a quote without searching.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Full Service Display" icon="list-check">
    Show everything you offer. Pull your services from Google Business Profile and add detail. Consumers see a complete picture of your business in one place.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Social Profiles" icon="share-nodes">
    Connect your social media pages. Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and other profiles linked directly from your Hometrics listing.
  </Card>
  <Card title="SEO Backlink" icon="link">
    Every claimed profile includes a backlink to your website. Indexed by search engines, helping your online presence beyond the directory.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
