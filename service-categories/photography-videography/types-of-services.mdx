Real estate photography services range from basic still photography to comprehensive multimedia packages. Understanding service options helps clients choose appropriate visual marketing for their property and budget.

Most photographers offer multiple services and package combinations.

## Still Photography

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Standard interior and exterior photography">
    Core photography service for listings:
    
    **What's included:**
    - 20-40 high-resolution photos (varies by package and property size)
    - Interior shots of all main rooms
    - Exterior front and back views
    - Key features and details
    - Professional editing and color correction
    - Digital delivery (download link or gallery)
    
    **Typical approach:**
    - Wide-angle lens to show full rooms
    - Multiple angles of important spaces
    - HDR or exposure blending for balanced lighting
    - Vertical and horizontal orientations as appropriate
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Twilight or dusk photography">
    Photos taken during golden hour or after sunset:
    
    **What makes it special:**
    - Warm, inviting exterior lighting
    - Interior lights visible through windows
    - Pool and landscape lighting highlighted
    - Sky with color and drama
    - Distinguished, upscale appearance
    
    **Best for:**
    - Homes with attractive exterior lighting
    - Properties with pools or outdoor features
    - Luxury listings
    - Homes with appealing evening ambiance
    
    Typically offered as add-on to standard photography package.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Detail and feature shots">
    Close-up photography of specific elements:
    
    - Architectural details
    - High-end finishes and materials
    - Appliances and fixtures
    - Custom features
    - Craftsmanship
    
    Highlights quality and upgrades. Often included in standard packages or available as add-on.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Video Services

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Property walkthrough video">
    Guided video tour of property:
    
    **What's included:**
    - Smooth, professionally shot footage
    - Logical flow through property
    - 1-3 minute edited video
    - Background music
    - Transitions between scenes
    - Text overlays (optional)
    - Optimized for social media and websites
    
    **Filming approach:**
    - Gimbal-stabilized camera for smooth motion
    - Thoughtful pacing and timing
    - Highlighting key selling features
    - Showing flow and layout
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Cinematic property film">
    Elevated video production:
    
    - Higher production value
    - Multiple camera angles and shots
    - Drone footage integration
    - Professional color grading
    - Music and sound design
    - Lifestyle elements or storytelling
    - 2-5 minute feature presentation
    
    Used for luxury properties or significant marketing campaigns.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Agent introduction video">
    Short video of listing agent:
    
    - Welcome and property introduction
    - Key features highlighted
    - Call to action
    - 30-60 seconds typically
    
    Adds personal connection and agent branding.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Social media content">
    Short-form video for social platforms:
    
    - Instagram Reels or Stories
    - TikTok format
    - Facebook video posts
    - 15-60 seconds
    - Vertical format optimization
    - Trending music or styles
    
    Designed for maximum engagement on social media.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Drone Services

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Aerial photography">
    Still photos from elevated perspectives:
    
    **What's captured:**
    - Bird's-eye view of property
    - Roof condition visible
    - Lot size and boundaries
    - Neighborhood context
    - Proximity to amenities
    - Landscaping and outdoor spaces
    
    **Best for:**
    - Large lots or acreage
    - Waterfront properties
    - Homes with extensive grounds
    - Properties with desirable locations
    - Unique architectural elements best seen from above
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Aerial video">
    Drone video footage:
    
    - Smooth flyover or reveal shots
    - Orbiting property views
    - Ascending or descending shots
    - Neighborhood context
    - Integrated into property video
    
    Adds cinematic quality to property marketing.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Legal and safety requirements">
    Drone operation requirements:
    
    - Pilot must hold FAA Part 107 certification
    - Comply with local airspace regulations
    - Property owner permission required
    - Neighbor notification advisable
    - Weather and visibility considerations
    - No-fly zones must be avoided (airports, restricted areas)
    
    Verify photographer has proper licensing and insurance.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Virtual Tours

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="3D virtual tours (Matterport)">
    Immersive 3D property experience:
    
    **What's provided:**
    - 3D walkthrough of entire property
    - Room-to-room navigation
    - Dollhouse view showing full layout
    - Floor plan view
    - Measurement tools
    - Tag features and information
    - Shareable link and embed code
    
    **Technology:**
    - Matterport or similar 3D camera system
    - Multiple scans throughout property
    - Software processes into 3D model
    - Hosted on platform
    
    **Best for:**
    - Out-of-area or international buyers
    - High-end properties
    - Vacant properties
    - Investment properties
    - Providing thorough remote viewing
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="360-degree photography">
    Interactive panoramic photos:
    
    - Full 360-degree view of each room
    - Click to navigate between rooms
    - Less expensive than 3D tours
    - Faster to create
    - Interactive but not full 3D
    
    Middle ground between static photos and full 3D tours.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Virtual tour platforms">
    Common platforms and technologies:
    
    - Matterport (most popular for 3D)
    - iGuide
    - Zillow 3D Home
    - CloudPano
    - Ricoh Theta (360-degree)
    
    Platform choice affects features, quality, and hosting.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Specialty Services

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Virtual staging">
    Digitally adding furniture to empty rooms:
    
    - Furniture digitally inserted into photos
    - Various style options
    - Shows potential of empty spaces
    - More affordable than physical staging
    - Must be disclosed in listings
    
    See Staging section for detailed information.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Floor plans">
    2D layout drawings:
    
    **Types:**
    - Basic black and white layouts
    - Color-coded room designations
    - Dimensioned plans with measurements
    - 3D rendered floor plans
    
    **Creation methods:**
    - Measured during photo shoot
    - Created from architectural plans
    - Generated from 3D tour data
    
    Helps buyers understand layout and flow.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Photo editing and enhancement">
    Post-processing services:
    
    - Sky replacement
    - Grass greening
    - Clutter removal
    - Virtual renovations
    - Object removal
    - Day-to-dusk conversion
    - Color correction
    
    Should enhance, not misrepresent property.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Branded marketing materials">
    Property marketing creation:
    
    - Property brochures
    - Feature sheets
    - Postcards and flyers
    - Social media graphics
    - Email headers
    - Property websites
    
    Photographers may offer design services or partner with designers.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Package Options

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Basic packages">
    Entry-level photography:
    
    - 15-25 photos
    - Interior and exterior coverage
    - Basic editing
    - Standard turnaround
    
    Suitable for smaller homes, lower price points, or budget constraints.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Standard packages">
    Most common for typical listings:
    
    - 25-40 photos
    - Comprehensive interior and exterior coverage
    - Professional editing
    - May include twilight shots
    - Faster turnaround
    
    Appropriate for most residential sales.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Premium packages">
    Comprehensive visual marketing:
    
    - 40+ photos
    - Video walkthrough
    - Drone photography or video
    - Twilight photography
    - Virtual tour
    - Rush processing
    - Marketing materials
    
    Used for higher-end properties or competitive markets.
  </Accordion>

## Choosing Services

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Consider property type">
    Match services to property characteristics:
    
    **Small condo:** Basic photography may suffice
    **Single-family home:** Standard package with exterior and interior
    **Large estate:** Premium package with drone and video
    **Vacant property:** Consider virtual staging
    **Unique features:** Video to show flow and character
    **Acreage:** Drone photography essential
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Consider market and price point">
    Higher price points typically warrant more comprehensive visual marketing:
    
    - Buyers expect professional presentation
    - Competition may have extensive marketing
    - Investment justifiable relative to sale price
    - Out-of-town buyers may require virtual tours
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Consider marketing strategy">
    Align with overall marketing approach:
    
    - Social media focus: Video and short-form content
    - Out-of-area buyers: Virtual tour essential
    - Luxury positioning: Premium photography and video
    - Quick sale goal: Comprehensive visual marketing
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
