# Welcome to the Hometrics Guide

Real estate transactions involve multiple professionals you've likely never hired before. Most people accept whoever their agent recommends without independent verification. This guide exists so you don't have to.

The Hometrics Guide explains every service category involved in real estate transactions. Use it to understand what each professional does, what to evaluate when hiring, and what questions to ask.

## How to Use This Guide

**Looking up a term?** Start with the [Glossary](/overview/helpful-tools/glossary). Quick definitions with links to detailed guides.

**Researching a service?** Go to [Service Categories](/service-categories/title-escrow/overview). Each guide explains what to look for and red flags to avoid.

**Ready to compare?** Search the [Directory](https://hometrics.io) to find local professionals with verified reviews.

## What You'll Find

- 13 service category guides covering title companies, lenders, agents, inspectors, and more
- Educational content explaining every transaction stage
- Checklists for comparing providers
- Definitions of industry terms
- Neutral guidance that assumes most professionals are competent and ethical

You don't need industry experience to use this guide. If you can ask questions, you can make informed decisions.
