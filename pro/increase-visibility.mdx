## What Reduces Visibility

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Long gaps in activity">
    Businesses with months between reviews appear inactive or inconsistent.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Incomplete profiles">
    Missing information creates uncertainty. Customers skip profiles that lack basic details.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Slow response times">
    Ignored inquiries or slow follow-through signal low prioritization of customer engagement.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Unclaimed profiles">
    Unclaimed profiles rank lower and miss opportunities for visibility improvements.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
