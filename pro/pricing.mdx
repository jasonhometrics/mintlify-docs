## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="Is this for individual agents or solo professionals?">
Hometrics is built for businesses with consistent customer volume. Most customers process 10+ transactions per month. Individual agents with low volume are generally not a fit.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do you post on my Google Business Profile?">
No. Hometrics manages reputation and engagement but does not create posts or content.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I respond to my own reviews?">
Yes. You always retain full access to your Google Business Profile. Respond anytime, or let Authority handle responses for you.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is this SEO or local search?">
No. Hometrics is reputation management. The Authority plan includes listings management for NAP consistency, but does not include SEO services.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I cancel anytime?">
Yes. Monthly plans have no long-term contracts. Enterprise agreements may have different terms.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What integrations are available?">
Hometrics integrates with most CRMs and transaction management systems. [Contact us](/contact) to confirm compatibility.
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
