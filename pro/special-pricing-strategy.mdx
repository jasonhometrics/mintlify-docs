---
title: "Special Pricing"
description: "How to offer discounts as trust signals without commoditizing your service"
---

Special pricing signals appreciation, community alignment, and long-term relationships. Customers filter by discount categories when searching. Select the ones that genuinely reflect what you offer. 

<Warning>
  Pricing discounts are not required and do not impact your profile ranking.
</Warning>

---
